Our Story

Adval Specialty is a technocrat-led organization committed to excellence in the chemical manufacturing industry. Founded on the principles of innovation and integrity, we specialize in delivering high-performance solutions tailored to the unique needs of our sector.

Our team of dedicated technocrats combines deep industry knowledge with cutting-edge research to create products that drive efficiency and sustainability. From our humble beginnings to becoming a key player in multiple industrial segments, our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of adding value.

2013 Adval Organics Pvt. Ltd. formed.
2015 Polymerisation unit commissioned (Sheela Chemicals).
2020 AKD Emulsion Plant commissioned.
